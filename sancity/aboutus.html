Founder & Visionary

Founded 2006

Dr. Vishwa Cariappa B.S.

Computer Engineer · Founder, SAN Group of Companies

SAN City, formerly known as VSan Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd. (SAN Group of Companies), was founded in 2006 by computer engineer Dr. Vishwa Cariappa B.S.

Since its inception, SAN has been building homes and businesses throughout Karnataka. Its completed projects include those in Bangalore, Mysore, Chikkaballapur, and Coorg. Under the brand and identity of San Properties & Developers — which subsequently changed its name to “VSan Infrastructure Pvt. Ltd.” — SAN has been delivering quality on all fronts constantly.
